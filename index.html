Solar Energy

Solar energy is harnessed from the Sun using photovoltaic (PV) cells or solar thermal systems.

Hydraulic (Hydropower) Energy

Hydropower generates electricity using the movement of water, typically from dams, rivers, or ocean currents.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is generated by converting the kinetic energy of wind into electricity using wind turbines.

Microhydro Power Plants

A microhydro power plant generates electricity from the flow of water, ideal for rural areas where grid electricity may not be available.

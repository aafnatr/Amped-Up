Solar Energy

Solar energy is harnessed from the Sun using photovoltaic (PV) cells or solar thermal systems. It is widely used for electricity generation, heating, and industrial applications.

Hydraulic (Hydropower) Energy

Hydropower generates electricity using the movement of water, typically from dams, rivers, or ocean currents. It is one of the oldest and most widely used renewable energy sources.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is generated by converting the kinetic energy of wind into electricity using wind turbines.
